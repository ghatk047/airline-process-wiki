Airline-Specific Risks & Pain Points

Point-to-point network: any route change propagates aircraft + crew impacts across all airports simultaneously
No alliance = fewer slot-swap options at congested airports vs legacy carriers
Crew feasibility (Phase 4): New crew system still maturing — single highest-risk step in the process
Cross-dept review (Phase 4) is longest elapsed step: 4–6 weeks typical with multiple re-loop cycles
